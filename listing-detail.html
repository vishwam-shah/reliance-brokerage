Business Overview

The Golden Fork is an award-winning fine dining establishment located in the heart of Kuala Lumpur City Centre, operating at full capacity since 2013. The restaurant is renowned for its Modern European cuisine with Malaysian influences, attracting a loyal base of corporate clients, expatriates, and discerning local diners.

With 120 covers across two dining floors and a private events space accommodating up to 40 guests, the business benefits from high yield per seat and consistent repeat patronage. The kitchen operates under a Michelin-recognised Head Chef with a team of 18 culinary professionals.

The current owner is pursuing a strategic exit to focus on other ventures. All senior management have been briefed and are committed to continuity post-transition.
